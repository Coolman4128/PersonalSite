About Me

I am a life-long lover of computers who has always wanted to make them do what he wanted. While I am still figuring out how to do that, I get a little better at it every day. My dream is to use that passion to build things that will excite people.

I was born in rural Pennsylvania, and through the loggers and farmers I found my way to computers. I go to Penn State University for my bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, and have worked on many projects over the years. My projects range from intricate backend systems simple little projects to make my life better. Whether it is designing databases or soldering to arduinos I thrive on solving problems.
